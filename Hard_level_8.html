16x16 Rules (4x4 Boxes)

  • Row RuleEach row must contain the numbers 1 through 16, without repetition.
  • Column RuleEach column must also contain the numbers 1 through 16, without repetition.
  • Box RuleEach of the sixteen $4 \times 4$ sub-grids must contain the numbers 1 through 16, without repetition.

How to Play

  1. Click on an empty cell to select it.
  2. Click a number from the number pad below the grid to place it.
  3. Use the 'Clear' button or **Backspace/Delete** to remove a number.
  4. **Keyboard shortcuts: 1-9 for single digits, A/B/C/D/E/F/G for 10-16**
  5. Fill all cells correctly to win the game!
